01 — Foundation

Range, held together by a way of thinking.

You build across improbably different fields — EU foreign-policy news, a Zen seasonal calendar, a life-design framework, a city's fitness schedule — and turn that curiosity into small, finished software. The brand makes that breadth read as depth.

Positioning

Independent builder. Designs and ships small, sharp web products across policy, culture, and wellbeing.

Personality

PreciseLiterate Quietly playfulHuman

Voice

Plain and exact. Short sentences. Explains, never sells. Wit used like seasoning, not the meal.

Principles

  1. Type does the work.
  2. One accent, used with intent.
  3. Range is the structure.
  4. Warm details, rigorous frame.
